Service and distribution channels are delivering incremental growth and are being supported by enhanced leadership.
Management stated backlog does not include potential new wins from data center products, suggesting possible upside to guidance if opportunities are realized during the year.
Guided revenue is expected to be "relatively evenly" distributed across quarters, reducing seasonality concerns for investors monitoring results intra-year.
INDUSTRY GLOSSARY
ESCO channel: Energy Service Company channel; a business model in which an external provider designs, installs, and often finances energy-saving projects for customers.
Turnkey services: Comprehensive project fulfillment covering all aspects from design and installation to commissioning and service, requiring no additional vendors by the client.
Voltrek earn-out: Contingent payments related to the prior acquisition of Voltrek, triggered upon certain post-acquisition performance milestones.
